About this Faculty

The faculty of applied science and technology run bachelor of technology,bachelor of science,HND and certificate programmes. The faculty trains students in science, technology and other related fields with the objective of turning out professionals both for industry and academia. The vision of the Faculty of Applied Science will strive to be a dynamic center of innovation and creativity dedicated to teaching, lifelong learning, professionalism, research, entrepreneurship, and partnership with local and global communities.
